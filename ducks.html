DUCKS

Duck is the common name for numerous species of waterfowl in the family Anatidae. Ducks are generally smaller and shorter-necked than swans and geese, which are also members of the same family.

DISEASES

Duck Virus hepatitis

Duck Plague (Duck Virus Enteritis)

Riemerella anatipestifer Infection.

Avian Cholera.

Colibacillosis.

SYMPTOMS

Affected ducklings become lethargic,lose balance, paddle spasmodically, and die within minutes, typically with opisthotonos.
